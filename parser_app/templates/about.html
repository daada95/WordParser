This is a Word Parser App.

App created for students to make learning great again.

How to use that web app:

  • Register & login in website,
  • Get the .docx (Microsoft Word) document with material you want to learn,
  • Make sure that your document fits into requirements (below),
  • Upload the document into the parser,
  • Get your flashcards and start learning!

Requirements of document to correct upload:

Fonts:

  • Category: Title font,
  • Title: Heading 1 font
  • Content: Normal font

Other:

  • Document should contain only one category,
  • Content of the flashcard have to be unique,

Warning! If your document won't fit those requirements, parser will raise errors, and upload won't be done.
